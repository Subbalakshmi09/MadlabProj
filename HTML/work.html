Shift Click

Shift Click fulfils a long-held ambition – needed now more than ever – to create a high-impact end-to-end pipeline for young people under-represented in the digital industry in Stockport: from first steps through to confident career growth.

Make Stuff2

Make Stuff (2) was a creative place-making festival delivered in central Stockport between December 2023 and May 2024. Developed in collaboration with residents, it took place at community venues across the town. Supported by the UK Government Shared Prosperity Fund, it delivered 125 activities (totalling 330 hours) to over 1700 people.
